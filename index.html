Michel Georges Tabet

Michel Georges Tabet is the CEO and Co-Founder of FinanceMe, an independent Investment Management boutique that is focused on providing investment services to private clients and professional investors. Despite his age, Michel has a lot of experience in the professional world.

Education

Michel began his studies in 2017, at ESA Business School in Lebanon. After being selected by his university, he has joined the prestigious university of SciencesPo Paris for an exchange semester. As part of his academic cursus, he is actually ending his studies at ESSEC Business School in France, where he will acquire a double degree diploma in Business Administration with a Master1 in Management.

Experience

Michel has done many internships before becoming a young entrepreneur. From 2017 to 2020, he managed to work in many financial firms. First, he has joined the london Management Group, a Wealth Management company in London, then Midis Group, a Financial Services & Advisory company in Bahrain. In 2020, Michel has completed an internship at The EuroMena Funds, a Private Equity company based in the Middle East region.

Additional Information

For Michel: "Being an entrepreneur doesn't mean you can't enjoy your life". Michel was a professional football player in the Lebanese 3rd Division before joining the ESSEC's Men Soccer team. Moreover, he is a world traveler. He has visited more than 30 countries all over the world.
